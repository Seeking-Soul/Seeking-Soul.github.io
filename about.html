About Us

Seeking S.O.U.L

In seeking for our souls, for our purpose, it’s easy to get lost along the way. We tend to, almost compulsively, reach for things outside of ourselves; and will utilize those things until their substance runs dry, or we become disillusioned with their initial promise. Of course, the answer to all of our questions is within. To turn within allows us to become still, to become no longer preoccupied with the outside world, and to cultivate an awareness of the universal love within our own hearts. This is our purpose. To learn to love ourselves, and in time, turn that love outwards towards the phenomenal world. This love, the love that comes from deep within, is unconditional. To live life from a place of unconditional love is to See the One Universal Love that is always present in everything that we perceive, think, feel, do, experience, etc. There is a quiet awareness behind all phenomena that does not fear, that does not judge, that resides entirely in the present moment. To act in life from this place of quiet awareness is to become a beacon of Universal Love. That is the purpose of Seeking SOUL. To help everyone see that they are already enough, and that within them is exactly what they have been searching for. We hope to do this through as many modalities as we can, whether it’s Reiki, energy work, massage therapy, inner child healing, ceremony, guided meditation, or spiritual coaching. Our mission is to make the world a better place. Please join us in being the change we all want to see in the world.

Brian has been practicing energy work for over 2 years. Including practices such as Reiki, Qi Gong and a tactile-imaging technique used in meditation. He is a certified Reiki practitioner (Level 1), having tutored under Li Heping; a Medical Qigong Master, Reiki Master, and Ordained Daoist Priest. Under Li’s mentorship, Brian has been able to gain deeper knowledge into his own innate gift using energy, expanding upon his already incredible sensitivity and skill.

Having been deeply committed to self development since his mid teens, Brian also has a wealth of knowledge to share regarding emotional wellbeing. He has spent his adult life teaching what he knows to those that will listen, and now coupled with his spiritual wisdom, is dedicated to helping others find peace within. He offers spiritual life coaching based entirely on what he knows works and will get results. He offers a very practical approach to all areas of self development and spiritual practice. There is no fluff or filler, because to him, the ultimate goal is to live a happy and fulfilling life. He believes that the more people he can help find a more sustainable happiness, the better the world will become.
